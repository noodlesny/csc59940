About Us

We are a group of like-minded students that get together to exchange items with each other.
Books, clothes, your grandma's old tube TV- it's all fair game. If you can get it here, they can get it out!

Who should join? Anyone that likes:

  • Reducing waste and giving useful items a second chance
  • Finding treasure in others' trash
  • Getting to know students that aren't in their forced class groups
  • Minimizing their risk of ending up on Hoarders

Swap Times:

Tuesdays during club hours-- 12:30p - 1:15p

*Weekend swaps are also scheduled throughout the semester.
